Peace Hoziyana yasezerewe muri East Africa’s Got Talent

Umunyarwandakazi Peace Hoziyana yasezerewe mu irushanwa ry’abanyempano muri Afurika y’Iburasirazuba rizwi nka East Africa’s Got Talent, ririmo kubera i Nairobi muri Kenya
. Ku nshuro ya mbere ribaye, u Rwanda ni kimwe mu bihugu byemerewe kuryitabira aho ruhatanye n’ibindi birimo Uganda, Tanzania na Kenya. Ryitabiriwe n’abanyempano batandukanye bo mu bihugu bigize
EAC, ariko 18 gusa ni bo bemerewe kujya muri ½, muri aba hakabamo bane baturutse mu Rwanda. Ku ikubitiro, mu bahatanye muri iki cyiciro abaturutse mu Rwanda barimo Elisha The Gift na Himbaza Drummers bahise basezererwa. Hari hasigayemo babiri aribo Itorero Intayoberana na Peace Hoziyana [wize umuziki ku Nyundo.]
Tariki 22 Nzeri 2019 nibwo Itorero Intayoberana ryatambutse imbere y’akanama nkemurampaka aho zanabashije kubona itike yo gukomeza mu cyiciro cya nyuma cy’irushanwa, mu gihe Peace Hoziyana we yasezerewe kuri iki Cyumweru tariki 29 Nzeri 2019.
Peace Hoziyana yanyuze imbere y’abagize akanama nkemurampaka ari uwa kabiri, afite iminota ibiri yo kwiyerekana cyane ko ariyo igenerwa buri wese muri iki cyiciro. Peace Hoziyana ntabwo yahiriwe n'irushanwa Makeda niwe gusa wabashije kumutora
